Adware

What is Adware?

Adware is software that automatically displays or downloads advertising material, often in the form of pop-ups or banners. While sometimes legitimate, it often installed without clear consent.

How It Works

  • Displays unwanted advertisements
  • Tracks browsing to target ads
  • Redirects search queries
  • Slows down computer performance
  • Difficult to remove

Real-World Example

DeskAd (2015-2020): Adware that injected advertisements into web browsers and could also download additional malware. It affected millions of users worldwide through seemingly legitimate software.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Carefully read installation prompts
  • Choose custom installation
  • Decline bundled toolbars/apps
  • Use ad-blocker extensions
  • Remove unwanted programs from control panel
