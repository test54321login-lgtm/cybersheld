Spyware

What is Spyware?

Spyware is a type of malware that secretly monitors your computer usage and collects information about you without your knowledge. It often runs in the background undetected.

How It Works

  • Tracks browsing habits and keystrokes
  • Collects passwords and login credentials
  • Gathers personal and financial information
  • Monitors email and chat conversations
  • Shares data with remote attackers

Real-World Example

CoolWebSearch (2002-2004): A notorious spyware program that hijacked web browsers, changed homepages, and redirected searches. It was difficult to remove and infected millions of computers.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Use anti-spyware software
  • Be careful with free software downloads
  • Read privacy policies
  • Don't click on suspicious pop-ups
  • Keep your browser updated
