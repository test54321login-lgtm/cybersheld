Phishing

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of social engineering attack where attackers impersonate trustworthy entities to trick victims into revealing sensitive information like passwords, credit cards, or personal data.

How It Works

  • Sends fake emails appearing legitimate
  • Creates fake login pages
  • Uses urgency and fear tactics
  • Targets individuals or organizations
  • Steals credentials or installs malware

Real-World Example

Google and Facebook Wire Transfer Scam (2013-2015): Attackants impersonated a vendor and sent fake invoices, tricking both companies into导线 transferring over $100 million. One of the largest single phishing scams in history.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Check sender email addresses carefully
  • Hover over links to see actual URLs
  • Never provide passwords via email
  • Verify requests through official channels
  • Use anti-phishing browser extensions
